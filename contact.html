FAQ

Common questions.

What services does ArtoryX offer?

We offer Web Solutions (responsive static websites, landing pages, full-stack platforms), Graphic Design (brand identity, event posters, social media creatives, digital art), and AI-Powered Platforms (custom web apps with AI/Deep Learning integration).

How long does a project typically take?

Web projects are typically completed within 1–3 weeks. Graphic design projects take 3–7 days. AI platform builds take 3–6 weeks depending on complexity. We'll provide a clear timeline during initial consultation.

What's included in pricing?

Pricing is project-based and includes design, development, revisions, and deployment. We provide detailed quotes upfront with no hidden fees.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. We offer post-launch support including bug fixes, content updates, and performance optimization to keep your project running smoothly.

Can you work with remote clients?

Absolutely. We work with clients worldwide using modern communication and project management tools for seamless collaboration.
