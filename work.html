Daichiro Professional Skills Academy

daichiro.in

Daichiro Professional Skills Academy is an Imphal-based education company offering professional skill training, career counselling, and AI-driven career assessment tools. ArtoryX architected and built their entire digital platform from the ground up — delivering a full-stack, cloud-hosted web experience that powers their business operations.

The platform includes a dynamic service pages system, student-facing career report PDFs, an admin management dashboard, and integrated AI intelligence — all deployed on AWS EC2 for maximum reliability.
